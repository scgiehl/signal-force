# Signal Force Capabilities Page Prompt

Revise the Signal Force capabilities page so it communicates both the product architecture and the intelligence value behind it.

The current “Decision Ready Intelligence” direction is good, but I want the page to make it clearer that Signal Force is an AI powered GovCon intelligence engine, not just a set of reports or separate products.

## Important Positioning

Signal Force should feel like one platform and one analytical engine. It should not feel like five separate products, five separate SKUs, or a menu of disconnected tools.

The page should explain that Signal Force uses artificial intelligence to pull together and analyze multiple categories of GovCon growth intelligence, including:

- Market data
- Agency priorities
- Opportunity signals
- Teaming context
- Company positioning
- News
- Emerging technologies
- Budget movement
- Funding signals
- Incumbent information
- Contract history
- Prime and subcontractor ecosystems
- Relationship data
- Live people and company context
- Decision maker and influencer mapping
- Modern GovCon BD practices
- Capture strategy patterns
- Post award teaming opportunities

## Key Message

Signal Force reads the market the way an experienced BD operator would, but at AI scale.

It combines data, context, relationships, timing, agency demand, company fit, and practical BD methodology to produce decision ready intelligence.

This is not a CRM.
This is not a generic market research tool.
This is not just AI generated copy.
This is a GovCon growth intelligence engine built to help teams decide where to focus, who to target, what to pursue, how to team, and what to do next.

### 4. The Reads

Keep the same five intelligence outputs currently on the page, but reframe them as workflows from the engine, not product tiers.

Do not use giant 01 to 05 numerals.

Use small mono report codes like:

- R 01
- R 02
- R 03
- R 04
- R 05

These should feel like intelligence report types, not separate products.

Each read should answer:

- What decision does this support?
- What does the AI analyze?
- What does the user walk away with?

**Example format:**

#### R 01 / Agency Fit Read

**Decision:**  
Should we target this agency?

**Signal Force analyzes:**  
Agency mission, budget priorities, active programs, modernization themes, recent awards, incumbent landscape, technology demand, and company fit.

**Output:**  
A focused agency fit brief showing where the company has a credible angle, where it does not, and what to do next.
